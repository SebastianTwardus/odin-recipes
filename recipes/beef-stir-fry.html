Beef Stir-Fry

Description

This classic recipe will make good use of leftover ingredients and produce a healthy meal in just 25 minutes. Packed with nutrients and flavour this dish is a household staple that will not disappoint.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Beef Sirloin
  • Broccoli Florets
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Carrots
  • Green Onion
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. In a large skillet heat vegetable oil over medium-hight heat.
  2. Cook the beef for 3-4 minutes until browned.
  3. Add vegetables and garlic to the skillet and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Season with soy sauce, top with sesame seeds, and finish cooking when vegetables are tender.
  5. Enjoy!
