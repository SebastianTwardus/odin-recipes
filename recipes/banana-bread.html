Banana Bread Recipe

Description

This recipe will create a moist and delectable loaf of banana bread. It's packed full of banana flavour that will be a sure hit with friends and family.Try it toasted for a novel taste experience.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 °C
  2. In one bowl combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl beat the butter and sugar using an electric mixer until smooth. Add eggs and mashed bananas until homogenous.
  4. Combine the banana mixture with the flour mixture and mix together
  5. Add the completed mixture to a lightly greased loaf pan
  6. Bake for about 60 minutes and let cool for 10 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
